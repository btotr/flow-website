About Flow recipes

Flow recipes is a new way of thinking about recipes. A recipe is composed of short instructions where you have ingredients that are prepared by methods like cutting or baking. This basic idea is the core of flow recipes. It exists of a visual language to express this idea as-well-as formal description to make recipes understandable by machines. This will make recipes easier to read, share and analyse. Apart from those benefits, the concepts behind flow recipes are metaphorical.
