Flow is metaphorical

Flow recipes is a wonderful idea on its own but serve a higher purpose as well. We live in an information age where technology is repeatedly evolving to a point where most humans hardly understand the impact. For example the lack of privacy, independency and transparency are common.

Flow recipes would be an exemplar on how we use technology for the good. Giving back ownership without compromises. Using something trivial and universal as recipes makes it easier to understand the power openness and the concept of a shared information space where we could collaborate on our own terms.
